Meet Amelia

Amelia Rivers crafts lyrical fantasy novels that celebrate found family, resilient love, and the courage to rewrite destiny. Her award-winning Luminous Cartography series has captivated readers in 27 countries with its richly imagined worlds and unforgettable characters.

When she’s not writing, Amelia mentors emerging authors, hosts storytelling workshops, and searches for indie bookstores wherever she travels.
